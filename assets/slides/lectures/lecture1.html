This course bridges theory and practice. Mathematical foundations give us the tools to analyze algorithms. We'll see real-world applications in machine learning, operations research, and engineering.

Example Formula

The optimization problem:

\[\min_{x \in \mathbb{R}^n} f(x) \quad \text{subject to} \quad Ax = b\]

This is the general form of a constrained optimization problem. f(x) is the objective function we want to minimize. The constraint Ax = b represents linear equality constraints. We'll spend several weeks understanding how to solve problems of this form efficiently.
